I have loved drawing since I was young. During primary school, I was classmates with my cousins, who are twins and excelled at everything, always securing the first and second places in class. Inspired by them, I started learning how to draw. However, at the age of 12, I moved to another school due to my parents' decision, and I couldn't learn drawing from them anymore. Later, I developed a crush on someone who liked anime, and when I saw a girl give him her drawing, I felt so excited. That motivated me to learn how to draw again to impress him. This journey started in 2017 and continues to this day.
